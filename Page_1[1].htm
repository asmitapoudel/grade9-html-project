Social Service
Social service is a professional and academic discipline
that seeks to improve the quality of life and well being of
an individual, group, or community by intervening through
research, policy, community organizing, direct practice, and
teaching on behalf of those afflicted with poverty or any real
or perceived social injustices and violations of their human
rights.Social workers are organized into local, national,
continental and international professional bodies. Social
work, an interdisciplinary field, includes theories from
economics, education, sociology, medicine, philosophy,
politics, anthropology and psychology.
